What is Cars24 B2B?

💡 Cars24 B2B is the supply-side procurement arm — Cars24 buys used cars directly from registered dealerships (NCD/UCD/DSA). When a customer trades in their car at a new car showroom, that vehicle comes to Cars24 B2B. This is the upstream supply engine feeding Cars24 inventory.
🟢 GS — Guaranteed Supply (80–85%)

Cars24 directly procures and pays the dealer. Car Jockey categorises the vehicle as GS at inspection. Dealer receives a Split Payment from the Other Charges / Other Hold bucket.

🔵 Non-GS — Auctioned (15–20%)

Vehicle is auctioned to third-party dealers. Cars24 facilitates but doesn't buy directly. Dealer incentive = MIN(2% × Base Amount, ₹10,000) per vehicle.

Pain Points — Root Causes & Business Impact

10 Identified
🔴

P1 — Dealer Onboarding: No Digital Form

RA collects data ad hoc via WhatsApp and paper. Sends to Central Ops by email. No status tracker. Missing documents cause repeated back-and-forth. TAT: 4–7 days per dealer.

✅ Fix: Panel-based digital form with document upload, validation, Central Ops queue, RA status tracker
🟡

P2 — Counter & UCM Mapping: Slack/WhatsApp Messages

Mapping managed manually by Central Ops. RA sends counter details via Slack message. Errors in mapping lead to wrong counter assignment, payout mismatches, and 3–7 day reversals downstream.

✅ Fix: System-stored, validated, immutable mapping at onboarding time. RA sees live counter status
🔴

P3 — SAP / Payee Creation: Google Form, Zero Validation

Wrong PAN, bad bank details, duplicates — all caught only during manual review. Adds 2–5 day delays before any payout can begin. VALIDATION_FAILED invisible to RA and TM until payment bounces.

✅ Fix: Integrated panel form with real-time validation — PAN uniqueness, duplicate payee check, bank detail verification
🔴

P4 — No Bucket Ledger: TM Guesses Balance

TM has zero visibility into Other Charges or Other Hold balances per App_ID. ~15% of requests are over-budget. Overpayments discovered only at release time — adds 1–2 days per occurrence.

✅ Fix: Real-time bucket ledger per App_ID. Balance displayed at request creation. System blocks submission if over-balance
🟡

P5 — Payout Combination Rules Not Enforced

Rules like "Split + Incentive not allowed on same App_ID" are informal knowledge. System doesn't enforce them. Invalid combinations raised and caught late — or missed entirely.

✅ Fix: System-enforced combination rules at request creation. Invalid combinations blocked before submission
🔴

P6 — Payout Requests via Email/WhatsApp

TM raises requests over email. No standard format. No audit trail. No structured approver queue. Requests get lost, delayed, or misrouted with no accountability. Average TAT: 2–5 working days even for ₹3,000.

✅ Fix: In-panel payout request. Structured form. 4-stage approval workflow. All actions logged immutably
🔴

P7 — No Approval Workflow: Email Forward Chains

BH receives 20–30 approval emails/day with no central view. Impossible to trace who approved what and when. Rejections provide no structured feedback. SLA is completely unknown and unmeasured.

✅ Fix: In-panel 4-stage workflow. Role-based routing. SLA timers. Mandatory rejection reasons. Full immutable audit log
🟡

P8 — Zero Management Visibility

Leadership has no real-time view of payout exposure, incentive burn, bucket utilisation, or pending approvals. Reporting via manual weekly collation — always stale by days.

✅ Fix: Real-time dashboards — payout exposure by stage/region, incentive burn vs. budget, pending approvals with SLA status
🟡

P9 — No App_ID History: TM Must Ask Central Ops Every Time

TM and RA cannot self-serve any information about a vehicle's payout status, UTR, rejection reason, or history. Every check requires messaging Central Ops or Finance.

✅ Fix: Full App_ID-level payout view — all requests, statuses, UTR, rejection reasons, bucket balances, approver history — fully self-serve
🟡

P10 — OEM Categorisation: Google Form, No Validation

Post-SI OEM categorisation submitted via Google Form. Zero system validation. Can be submitted with wrong category or missed entirely. Wrong categorisation directly impacts incentive eligibility.

✅ Fix: In-panel categorisation linked to dealer record. Validated by Central Ops. Full audit trail
